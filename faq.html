FAQ

Here are same of the frequently asked question

How do you send my order?

We ship all phones and laptops through trusted
delivery partners. Your order is packed safely and you
receive a tracking number to follow the
shipment in real-time until it reaches your door.

How to pick up the product?

You can pick up your device directly from our store.
After placing your order online, you will receive a
confirmation message. Show it at the shop and our team will prepare your pickup.

Can I return my order?

Yes, you can return your phone or laptop within
the return period if the item is unused and
in its original packaging. Returns for defective
products are fully covered by warranty.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 1–3 business days
depending on your location and product
availability. You will be updated by SMS or email
once your order is out for delivery.

Do devices have warranty?

All our laptops and phones come with
official manufacturer warranty, usually between
1–2 years depending on the brand. Warranty covers
hardware defects and factory issues.

Do you offer repairs?

Yes, we provide repair services for most laptop
and phone brands. We fix screens, batteries,
software issues, and hardware problems. Original
parts are used whenever possible.
